I am interested in algebraic number theory; in partuclar, the connection between Galois representations and automorphic forms. I am especially interested in the conjectures of Fontaine–Mazur and Langlands, and their interaction with the deformation theory of Galois representations, p-adic automorphic forms, and Selmer groups.
